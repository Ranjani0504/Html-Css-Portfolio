Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 350oF (180oC).
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl.
  3. Add chocolate chip and stir well.
  4. Bake for minutes or until golden brown.
Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Folour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Chocolate Chips 1 cup

Enjoy your homemade cookies!
